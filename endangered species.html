Endangered Species

Species that are on the verge of extinction due to the decreasing population on account of environmental or predatory factors

are refered to as Endangered

House Sparrow

Birds,our chirpy feathered friends, who have amazed us by their aerodynamics and colors, are under great threat. There are 9,934 species of birds that are existing in the world today, out of which a staggering 1,313 (13%) are threatened under extinction. House Sparrow is one of them.

Sparrows, though tiny, are very sensitive and strongly imune birds, and their sudden disaperance as sentimentals or as ecological indicators is a warning to humans about impending enviroental hazards.

Reasons for the dislocation of Sparrows

Non-availability of tiny insects as food due to the loss of vegetation

The excessive use of mosquito replents

Concrete architectures with no nesting sites for sparrows

Air-Conditioning which leaves no entry or exit points for feeding sparrow nestlings.
